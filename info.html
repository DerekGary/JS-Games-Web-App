Minesweeper Information

Welcome to Minesweeper! This page provides an overview of the game and instructions on how to play.

Overview

Minesweeper is a classic single-player puzzle game. The objective is to clear a rectangular board containing hidden "mines" or bombs without detonating any of them.

How to Play

  1. Click on a square to reveal what's underneath it.
  2. If a number appears, it indicates how many mines are adjacent to that square.
  3. Use the numbers to deduce the location of mines and mark them with flags.
  4. The game is won when all squares without mines are uncovered.
  5. Be careful! If you click on a square containing a mine, you lose the game.
