Minesweeper Information

Welcome to Minesweeper! This page provides an overview of the game and instructions on how to play and operate the game.

Overview

Minesweeper is a classic single-player puzzle game. The objective is to clear a rectangular board containing hidden "mines" or bombs without detonating any of them during the game.

How to Play

  1. Click on a square to reveal what's underneath it.
  2. If a number appears, it indicates how many mines are adjacent to that square in any direction.
  3. Use the numbers to deduce the location of mines and mark them with flags.
  4. The game is won when all squares without mines are uncovered and no mines have been detonated
  5. Be careful! If you click on a square containing a mine, you lose the game.
